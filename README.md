Business Pitch:
Problem the Website Solves
Problem	Solution Provided
No online presence	Professional website customers can find on Google
Lost walk-in customers	Clear address, map & opening hours visible instantly
Manual reservations only	Online table booking form on the website
Missing impulse orders	WhatsApp order button = direct, instant orders
No trust/credibility online	Reviews, story, gallery & awards build trust
Menu not visible online	Full digital menu with photos, names & prices
How It Helps the Business Grow
A website increases discoverability on Google Search, builds customer trust, enables 24/7 reservations, showcases the menu digitally, and drives WhatsApp orders — all without any recurring cost after launch.
